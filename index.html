Dissertation

“Computationally Efficient Forecasting via Gaussian Processes utilizing Large Datasets”

Supervised jointly by Dr Ben Calderhead, Department Of Mathematics, and Boussard and Gavaudan, a leading volatility-based hedge fund. I investigated the use of state-of-the-art Bayesian nonparametric statistical methodology developed by Binios et al. [paper] for making time series predictions. I found a novel application of treating short intervals of data as replicates; this allowed use of large financial datasets to their maximum potential.
